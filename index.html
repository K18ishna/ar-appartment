Project Overview

A premium semi-gated community apartment project located just 300 meters from Anutex, off the Warangal Highway, offering spacious 2BHK and 3BHK luxury flats ranging from 1120 to 1560 SFT. The five-floor project includes 65 exclusive East, West, and North-facing flats designed for natural light and ventilation. Premium amenities include a gym, kids play area, banquet hall, false ceiling throughout the flat, 24/7 security, CC cameras, power backup, and dedicated car parking. Construction is in progress with a February 2026 handover and 90% bank loan facility. Close to Uppal, ORR, metro, IT hubs, hospitals, and top educational institutions.
